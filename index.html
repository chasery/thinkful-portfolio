About

I work as a senior UX Designer, where I lead a team driving empathic design of software. My background in design and development fosters a collaborative environment between the two groups, resulting in the creation of the best product possible.

It’s that goal of finding the perfect solution for my end users’, that motivates me. Staying in tune with the latest software trends, I actively search for ways to employ modern approaches to my user centric solutions. I'm very passionate about usability and you shouldn’t be surprised if you hear me exclaim “That’s a bad user experience!”

My ability to quickly brainstorm suggestions for improvement has served me well in my fast-paced role. Being able to talk shop and work side-by-side with developers to rapid prototype, I have created a bond between design and development. This approach has fostered an environment amongst team members where everyone is focused on UX. I believe whether you are gathering requirements or you are writing backend services, you have a direct impact on the users of your software.
